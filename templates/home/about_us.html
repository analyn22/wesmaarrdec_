WESMAARRDEC

Twenty years ago, a group of 12 government agencies in Region 9 agreed to jointly undertake the establishment and management of the regional sectoral research and development consortium for Western Mindanao. The group is called Western Mindanao Agriculture and Resources Research and Development Consortium (WESMAARRDEC), with the Department of Agriculture IX (DA-IX) as the host agency from 1987-1996. On March 01, 1996, the stewardship of the consortium was passed-on to Philippine Coconut Authority-Zamboanga Research Center (PCA-ZRC). After four years, Western Mindanao State University (WMSU) is the one who hosted the consortium up to the present. Since its founding more than 28 years ago, WESMAARRDEC and its 23 member organizations have made significant advancements in coordinating regional research, development, and extension activities. These include planning, monitoring, and evaluating priority programs and related activities, coordinating R&D initiatives, and giving Western Mindanao's AANR sectors easy access to current technologies for increasing productivity and fostering sustainable development. In 2015, WESMARRDEC was renamed WESMAARRDEC as the result of unification of Aquatic and Agriculture R&D Institutions into the consortium structure.

The System

Archiving web-based application provides administrators or owners with an easy way to manage all the other related details of their users. We guarantee that our web-based application will be flexible and user-friendly, making it easier for different users to manage. The list of features of the application includes Administration which allows the management of users, monthly deletion of existing users, and existing files. The application also enables the upload and download of files. On the other hand, the User Panel permits the upload and download of files from the web-based application and searching for recent file uploads.
